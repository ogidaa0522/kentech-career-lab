What is KENTECH Career Lab?

KENTECH Career Lab is an AI-powered career guidance platform built specifically for students at Korea Institute of Energy Technology (KENTECH). It helps students prepare for job interviews and explore personalized career paths in the energy sector — including employment at public corporations, private companies, research institutes, and graduate school admission.

All AI features are powered by the Claude API (Anthropic), running entirely in the browser without a backend server.

Core Features

  • 🎤 AI Interview Question Generator — energy sector-specific technical & personality questions
  • 🧭 AI Career Recommendation — personalized top-3 career paths based on interests and strengths
  • ⚡ Energy Field Specialization — covers renewable energy, nuclear, hydrogen, grid, ESS, and more
  • 🌙 Dark Mode — toggle with preference saved to localStorage
  • 📱 Responsive Design — fully usable on mobile and desktop

Tech Stack

HTML5 CSS3 Vanilla JavaScript Claude API (Anthropic) localStorage API Google Fonts Vercel
